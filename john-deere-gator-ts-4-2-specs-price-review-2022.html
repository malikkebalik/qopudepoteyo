John Deere Gator Ts 4×2 Specs: John Deere’s TS 4×2 Gator is where the famous UTV line was born. The original design was for tough working capabilities.

The TS, as with all heavy-duty Gator models, has been constructed on a single-piece steel frame, which provides great durability and rigidity.

The John Deere Gator Ts 4×2 drivetrain operates on a continuous variable speed transmission with high and low speeds to improve performance.

Get more traction by activating the rear differential locking tires to provide the additional strength and torque you need to pull.

The TS Gator is a no-frills machine that places a lot of emphasis on durability and performance. With a payload capacity of 1,400 pounds, you can take advantage of a complete stone bed and more using the optional receiver hitch.

John Deere Gator Ts 4×2 Key Features

  • Top-of-the-line work capabilities
  • Stability is improved for better performance in challenging terrain
  • The best in class quality and durability
  • Freight is not included in the list prices.
