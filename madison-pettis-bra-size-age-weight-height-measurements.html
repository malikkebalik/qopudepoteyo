Biography:

Madison Pettis was born on July 22, 1998 in Arlington, Texas, U.S. Her first screen appearance was as Rocky Raccoon in the film Air Bud: Seventh Inning Fetch in 2002.

In 2006, she became known for her role as Bridget in Barney & Friends. The following year, she gained wider recognition for her role as Sophie Martinez in the Disney Channel Original Series Cory in the House.

She made a prolific career on television with other notable characters such as Allie Brookes in Life with Boys and Janelle in Lab Rats. In film, she rose to fame as Peyton Kelly in The Game Plan.
