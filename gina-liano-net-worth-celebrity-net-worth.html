What is Gina Liano's Net Worth?

Gina Liano is an Australian reality television personality who has a net worth of $10 million dollars. She is one of the cast members of the hit reality series, The Real Housewives of Melbourne. Liano works as a criminal barrister and is a single mom with two boys. She is viewed as the glue that holds her family together and she is the one that everyone calls when a crisis arises. She has been working in law for fourteen years and has represented some high-profile clients; but more times than not, you will find her in the courtroom fighting for the little guy.

She also loves fashion and is said to add to her wardrobe once a week. She loves to shop and updates not only her wardrobe, but also her home, in Versace's finest home interiors. In addition to working in law, she has also been a property developer and is a qualified art gallery curator. Looking her best is important to her and is known to get a spray tan every week, her hair and nails professionally maintained, and she is always dressed to impress. She recently launched her very own shoe line, is working on securing a book deal for her autobiography, and has become the new Ambassador for the Cancer Council of Victoria.
