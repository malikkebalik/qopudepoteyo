28 photos that show how Bella Thorne's style has evolved through the years

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1446 words  |  Larita Shotwell
Updated
  • Bella Thorne's style has evolved over the past 12 years. 
  • While she preferred punk pieces in her early teens, she has been gravitating toward daring, metallic looks lately. 
  • Visit Insider's homepage for more stories. 

Bella Thorne is known for her unique fashion sense. No matter how popular styles change, the actress — who became famous from her roles in TV shows like Disney Channel's "Shake It Up" and Freeform's "Famous in Love" — has found a way to put her own spin on any outfit.

When Thorne first hit the red carpet in 2008, at the age of 10, she favored punk pieces like plaid skirts and patterned ankle socks. Now 22, the star has a more trend-focused sense of style, favoring metallics, daring silhouettes, and bold makeup.

The singer and actress has undergone quite a dramatic style transformation over the years. Keep scrolling to see the surprising ways her style has changed.

Susanna Heller contributed to an earlier version of this story. 

In October 2008, Thorne wore a bell-sleeve jacket over a pink tutu-style dress to the "My Own Worst Enemy" premiere party.

She also wore her hair in curls.

The 11-year-old actress wore a metallic skirt to the premiere of "Jonas Brothers: The 3D Concert Experience" in February 2009.

She tied the look together with high socks.

In August 2010, the 13-year-old "Shake It Up" star wore a pink skirt to the ABC-Disney Television Critics Association party.

The actress styled her hair in curls.

She paired a cropped jacket with leggings and ankle boots at the September 2010 premiere of "You Again."

Thorne finished off the outfit with her signature ringlets and a large statement necklace.

In January 2011, Thorne went with a jewel-emblazoned beige dress for the Teen Choice Awards.

The actress and singer also wore hoop earrings and embellished sandals.

The 14-year-old actress wore an embellished teal dress and her hair in a side ponytail in July 2012.

She finished off her outfit with a pair of strappy heels. 

Thorne wore a collared black dress to the "Pitch Perfect" premiere in September 2012.

The actor also wore one bracelet on her wrist. 

In April 2013, aged 15, she wore a set of matching floral separates to the "Iron Man 3" premiere.

She styled her hair in a top knot with wispy pieces down in the front. 

In August 2013, Thorne wore a patterned shift dress for the premiere of "The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones."

She finished off her look with a loose braid and multicolored Christian Louboutin heels.

In April 2014, the 16-year-old actress wore a ruched dress with asymmetrical sleeves to the MTV Movie Awards.

Thorne wore her hair pin-straight to the event. 

In October 2014, she wore a mixed-material yellow dress to Elle magazine's Women In Hollywood event.

She also wore several gold bangles and beige heels.

The 17-year-old actress attended the January 2015 People's Choice awards in a black pantsuit.

She didn't wear too many accessories, allowing the suit to stand out. 

At the December 2015 premiere of "The Hateful Eight," the 18-year-old went with an edgier look when she paired a mixed-material mesh top with a floral miniskirt.

She kept her makeup simple, opting for a muted pink shade of lipstick and a coat of mascara.

In March 2016, the actress wore a cropped red sweater with a pair of matching pants to the Fast Company Grill.

She downplayed her otherwise bold outfit with understated white ankle boots.

Thorne wore a patterned, jewel-embellished midi dress to the March 2016 premiere of "Shovel Buddies" at South by Southwest.

She finished off her look with a pair of strappy metallic heels and a matching bracelet.

In December 2016, the 19-year-old wore a semi-sheer, mesh gown and plenty of eyeliner to the Critics' Choice Awards.

The only pop of color in this outfit came from her bright red hair, which she pulled into a sleek bun.

Thorne wore leather separates with an oversized cardigan, statement necklaces, beaded bracelets, and block heels to the Teen Choice Awards in August 2017.

Thorne completed her outfit by matching her makeup to her hair, both of which were pink. 

She attended the September 2017 Harper's Bazaar Icons party in an ombre gown with a mesh panel and feather details.

Thorne completed her outfit with an updo and several pieces of silver and diamond jewelry.

The next day, she wore a mermaid-style dress which featured a partial cutout and colorful embroidery to the Philipp Plein fashion show during New York Fashion Week.

She coordinated her eye makeup and lipstick to the details on her dress.

Thorne attended the September 2017 Rochambeau NYFW show wearing an all-pink ensemble, which consisted of a cropped sweatshirt, sweatpants, and ruched boots.

This look perfectly captured the 19-year-old's current style aesthetic: an original take on modern trends that feature a lot of glitter.

In December 2017, Thorne went for a sporty-casual look in a tracksuit-like ensemble for KIIS FM's Jingle Ball 2017 in Inglewood, California.

She topped off her look with silver jewelry, a piecey ponytail, and a rosy shade of pink lipstick.

Thorne wore a fringe shift dress with cutout details to the Sundance Film Festival in Park City, Utah, in January 2018.

With dark hair, she added a touch of color to the look with holographic lipstick.

The actress wore a mixed material gown to the world premiere of "Midnight Sun" in Hollywood, California, in March 2018.

The actress stuck to a rosy pink color scheme, from her hair to her makeup.

Thorne went for a more daring look at the premiere of "Assassination Nation" in Hollywood, California, in September 2018.

Thorne added a pop of color to her all-black ensemble with her red eye shadow.

Later that month, she went for a more casual look at the Halloween Horror Nights 2018 at Universal Studios Hollywood in Los Angeles.

She dressed up her otherwise casual outfit by layering several necklaces of mixed lengths. 

Thorne opted for a black mini dress with a dramatic neckline when she attended the premiere of Netflix's "The Dirt" in March 2019.

She added drama to her look with red winged eyeliner and a matching lipstick. 

She wore a plunging, metallic gown to the premiere of "Joker" at the 76th Venice Film Festival in August 2019.

The sheer dress also featured a thigh-high slit with a ruffle design on the hem. 

Bella Thorne rocked a silver metallic dress to the opening gala for the Oldenburg International Film Festival in September 2019.

Thorne kept her ombre hair down and drew attention to her eyes with glittery gold shadow.
