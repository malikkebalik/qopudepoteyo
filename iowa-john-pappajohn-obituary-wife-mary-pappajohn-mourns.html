John Pappajohn is a successful entrepreneur and philanthropist from Iowa who has significantly contributed to the state’s economic and charity organizations.

Pappajohn graduated from the University of Iowa with a bachelor’s degree in business in 1952 and became one of the University’s most distinguished graduates.

He is a very successful entrepreneur who has been involved in various profitable projects during his career.

In addition to his business accomplishments, Pappajohn is an active philanthropist. He has given tens of millions of dollars to Iowa charities, including education, the arts, and healthcare.

He has strongly supported the University of Iowa and received the Horatio Alger Award for charity in 1995.
