MiMa, 450 West 42nd Street, NYC - Rental Apartments

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  194 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The sleek, black-glass MiMa, developed by related Companies and designed by Arquitectonica, soars 63 story above Midtown West. Floor-to-ceiling windows offer dramatic, 360-degree panoramas of the city skyline, the Hudson River, the cliffs of the New Jersey Palisades, and vistas that stretch to the Appalachian Mountains on the horizon. In the units, gourmet kitchens come with quartz countertops, and while bathrooms feature Grigio Piove marble and quartz vanities. MiMA’s 44,000-square-foot amenity suite includes an Equinox fitness club, a heated indoor swimming pool, full-size indoor basketball and volleyball courts, a game room with billiards and card tables, a golf simulator, a tech center with a coffee bar, a children’s playroom, a spacious outdoor deck with a lush garden and BBQ grilling stations, an outdoor theater, an activity room for dogs, and more. The name MiMa stands for “middle of Manhattan,” an allusion to the building’s location at 10th Avenue and West 42nd Street, a short walk away from the Theater District, Hudson Yards, Hell’s Kitchen, and the park promenade at the Hudson River waterfront.
