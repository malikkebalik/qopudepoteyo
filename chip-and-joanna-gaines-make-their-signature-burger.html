Joanna Gaines is the star of her cooking show called Magnolia Table on Magnolia Network. The entrepreneur shared the screen with her husband Chip and together they made magic. Chip and Joanna shared the recipe for their signature burger but fans could only concentrate on their chemistry.

The Gaines burger that everyone wants to try

There’s no denying that Chip and Joanna make an incredible couple as fans have seen throughout the years on Fixer Upper. The stars play off of each other incredibly and make for great television. Although Magnolia Table features Joanna mainly, Chip often pops into a show here and there. The builder and the interior designer were seen making the Gaines Brothers Burgers on a recent episode that is making fans crave it.

“A burger and fries episode was long overdue—so @joannagaines’ kicking off an all-new season of #MagnoliaTable with help from @chipgaines as they make their signature Gaines Brothers Burger with a side of homemade crinkle-cut fries,” the post on Instagram read.

On Magnolia’s official website, Joanna details that the burger is part of the menu at Magnolia Table — the restaurant within the retail complex Magnolia Market in Waco, Texas.

“I have to confess that it’s one of my favorite things on the menu,” Joanna says on the website. “My boys love burgers of just about any kind, so I could probably get away with serving something a little plainer to them. I just love how a grilled buttered brioche bun holds a savory burger, sweet drip jam, creamy melted cheese, and peppery fresh arugula, so that every single bite is an amazing combination of flavors and textures.”

The Gaines Brother Burgers recipe

Chip and Joanna were generous to share their burger recipe online so everyone could enjoy the deliciousness of the Gaines Brother Burger. The recipe calls for 4 pounds of ground beef to be mixed with 1 tablespoon of kosher salt, and 1 tablespoon of pepper. When grilling the patties, you add the Gruyere cheese to each patty.

Next up will be the buns which will be brushed with a mix of garlic and butter to get them toasted. The website contains the recipe for two different types of “Drip Jam” that fans can add to their burgers.

Fans can only concentrate on Chip and Joanna

Although the burger recipe was delicious, fans could only concentrate on the sparks between Chip and Joanna. Fans adored seeing the couple cook together and make magic in the kitchen and their comments on the Instagram post were a reflection of that.

“Gotta love you two,” a fan replied.

“They’re so cute!” a follower added.

“Such a cute couple,” an Instagram user noted.

“The way they look at each other,” another fan mentioned.

“I just love how you two speak to one another… a lost art… humor, love, respect in the tone of your voices,” another follower commented.

“I just love you two. Your dynamic is a pleasure to watch. Best couple ever,” another Instagram user said.
