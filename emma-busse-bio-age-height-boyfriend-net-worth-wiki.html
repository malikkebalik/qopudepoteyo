Early Life

Emma Busse, born in 2003 and only 20 years old, completed her education last year. She has a degree in Musical Theater from the American Musical and Dramatic Academy, New York City. She gave her auditions in September last year for American Idol. Her mother’s name is Rina, and she also sings. Rina (the mother) is an officially trained singer and stage performer and teaches musical theater in Burnaby. Emma had her primary education at Notre Dame Regional Secondary School in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Emma Busse Career

She is an aspiring and emerging contestant on American Idol who belongs to Vancouver, British Columbia. Currently, she resides in New York City and is pursuing her passion for singing. Besides having a passion for singing, she is also a ballet lover and theater artist.

Back in 2020 (during her school days), she won the Notre Dame’s Got Talent competition and was also featured on the covers of Whitney Houston’s “Run to You” and “Jealous.” in her high school, she was featured with the lead role of Belle in the school’s production of “Beauty and the Beast.”
