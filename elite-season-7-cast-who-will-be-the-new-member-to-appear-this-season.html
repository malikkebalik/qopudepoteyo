Who Will Be in the Elite Season 7 Cast?

The cast for season 7 not yet confirmed. But we can expect that who will return in season 7. Let’s read about Elite Season 7 cast below.

Carla Daz

We can expect that Carla Daz will appear in Elite Season 7. Carla Carolina Moreira Diaz is a Brazilian actress and singer born on 28 November 1990. When she was just two years old, she started her career in television advertisements, where she eventually appeared in around eighty.

She first starred as Eliana on SBT’s soap serial Éramos Seis in 1994 and she rose to fame as the lovable Tininha at Colegio Brasil that same year. She first gained widespread recognition in 1997, when she starred as young Maria at the end of the first season of the novel Chiquititas Brasil. She remained with the show for a total of four seasons before leaving in 1999.

Martina Cariddi

Martina Cariddi will casted in Elite Season 7. Spanish-born Martina Basanta Cariddi born May 30, 2001 is a film and television actress best known for her role as Menca Blanco in the Netflix series Élite.

Martina’s parents, a Spaniard and an Italian gave birth to her on May 30th, 2001, in the Madrid neighbourhood of Chamartin. In 2010, she took her first step into the performing business by enrolling in a theatrical study at Madrid’s Escuela Municipal de Arte Dramático.

Later, she took acting classes at Cristina Rota’s Centro de Nuevos Creadores and studied dramatic painting at the Juan Codina Studio. In 2017, she made her acting debut in Fernando González Molina’s film El guardián invisible.

She played Laura in an episode of Season 19 of Cuéntame como paso that aired in 2018. A year later, she had a supporting role in Alejandro Amenábar’s feature film Mientras dure la guerra. She has a small role in a 2021 film by Luis Grajera. Father, you must die.

It was revealed in 2020 that she would reprise her role as Menca Blanco on the fourth season of the Netflix series Elite.

Manu Rios

Manu Rios will appear in Elite season 7. The Spanish actor and musician Manu Ros born December 17, 1998 is the same person that plays Patrick Blanco Commerford on the Netflix series Elite.

His musical career kicked off in 2010 when he began posting covers to YouTube. He started playing in the band Parchs in 2012 and since then they’ve published albums and toured extensively. In the city of Calzada de Calatrava, Ros entered the world on December 17th, 1998.

At the tender age of nine, he entered the world of show business by appearing in a reality show in Castilla-La Mancha. He also started posting his renditions of popular songs on YouTube around this time. He trained in both ballet and hip-hop and was a member of the reformed kids’ band Parchs in 2012, with whom he performed and recorded across Spain. In addition, he played Gavroche in a performance of Les Misérables.

Pol Granch

We can expect that Pol Granch will appear in Elite Season 7. Spanish-French musician and actor Pablo Grandjean better known as Pol Granch was born on April 4, 1998. Granch triumphed in the 2018 edition of the Spanish talent show Factor X Espaa, which was the show’s third season.

He was a member of Laura Pausini’s team and sang El sitio de mi recreo by Antonio Vega, “Pausa” by Izal and La quiero a morir by Francis Cabrel during the competition. His debut solo track titled “Late” and accompanying music video premiered in 2019. At this point, he started juggling other songwriting and recording projects with his established music career.

On April 26, 2019, he dropped his debut extended play, which he also titled. He released the first song from his debut studio album titled “Tengo que calmarme” on May 29, 2020. On June 26, 2020 they dropped the LP of the same name in its entirety.

In the same year it was reported that he was making the transition into acting with a major role as Philippe, a French royal in the fourth season of the Spanish Netflix series Élite. Granch’s “Tiroteo” single from 2021 featured guest vocals from Marc Segu and a remix by Rauw Alejandro.

His second studio album titled “Amor Escupido” was released in October 2022 and it was preceded by the singles “De Colegio”, “No Te Bastó, Mi Corazón”, “solo x te” and “Platicamos”.

Valentina Zenere

We can hopefully say that Valentina Zenere will appear in Elite Season 7. Valentina Zenere born on January 15, 1997 is an Argentine actress, model, and singer. She is most known for her roles as Alai Inchausti in the Argentine drama Casi ngeles as mbar Smith in the Disney Channel series Soy Luna and Juacas.

At a young age, Zenere began appearing in television commercials. She graduated from high school in 2014. Her big break came in 2010 when she was cast as Alai Inchausti in the hit drama Casi ngeles. After that she did a few little roles in theatre and on screen. She is most known for her role in Les Miserables.

The 2016 Disney Channel original series Soy Luna brought worldwide notice to Zenere. Her performance as the villain mbar Smith was praised by critics and audiences alike. In the Brazilian Disney Channel show Juacas, she played mbar once again. She retains close relationships to many of her co-stars, including Eugenia Suárez, Michael Ronda, Carolina Kopelioff, Gaston Vietto, Malena Ratner and Oriana Sabatini.

Andre Lamoglia

Brazil born actor Andre Lamoglia Agra Gomes born on August 4, 1997 is most known for his roles as Luan in Season 2 of Bia and Rafael Smor in Disney Channel Brazil’s Juacas. Thanks to his portrayal as Ivan on the Netflix series Elite, he gained more recognition around the world.

Carmen Arrufat

Spanish film and TV actress Carmen Arrufat Blasco was born on October 11, 2002. In recognition of her work in The Innocence, she was nominated for the Goya Award for Best New Actress. Thanks to her portrayal of a villain on the show HIT, she gained widespread recognition.

Arrufat began studying acting at the age of 11 at Vctor Antol’s Aula Cine y TV in Castellón de la Plana, where she went on to become a successful actress.  Arrufat was born on October 11, 2002.

In the 2019 drama The Innocence, Arrufat had her first appearance as a main character in a full-length film. She lied about her age in order to get the role. She was nominated for the Goya Award for Best New Actress because of her performance. After being cast as the narcissistic and deceitful Lena Vallejo in the Spanish version of HIT, she relocated to Madrid.
