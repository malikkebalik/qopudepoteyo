Lucien Laviscount is a British actor. He started his career in the early 2000s, debuting in an episode of Clocking Off. Some years later, he appeared in 3 episodes of Johnny and the Bomb. Later, he starred as Jake Briggs on the BBC children series Grange Hill. He gained wider recognition in the UK courtesy of his role as Ben Richardson on the long-running soap opera Coronation Street. Afterward, he starred as Jonah Kirby on Waterloo Road. Laviscount ultimately pursued a career in the US, debuting in an episode of The CW series Supernatural. He next played Brian in 3 episodes of Episodes and Earl Grey on Scream Queens. He recently starred as Billy Ayres on the short-lived show Snatch. In the movies, he had performances in One Night in Istanbul, The Bye Bye Man, and Love Beats Rhymes.
