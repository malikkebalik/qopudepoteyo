4 Burning Questions We Need Answers to This Season

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  552 words  |  Martina Birk

TV

Now that 'Floribama Shore' is back, fans are hoping these burning questions get answered at some point in season 4B.

Published on September 23, 2021

2 min read

Floribama Shore is back with all-new episodes. After the way things ended for the Floribama Shore cast in the first half of season 4, fans have several questions about the roommates, like what’s going on with Gus Smyrnios and Jeremiah Buoni? These are four burning questions that Floribama Shore fans need answers to in the second half of season 4. 

Is the feud between Gus Smyrnios and Jeremiah Buoni over? 

When Floribama Shore started, Gus and Jeremiah became fast friends. Known around the house as “JeriGus,” the duo was practically inseparable until season 3. 

Gus got frustrated after Nilsa Prowant brought a guest back to the beach house from the bar. When Jeremiah seemingly took the guest’s side instead of Gus’, the two got into a physical fight. 

That fight wasn’t the only thing to drive a wedge between the two. Gus and Jeremiah lived together outside of filming for Floribama Shore until Gus was “kicked out.” The tension from that situation and Jeremiah being hurt about the Floribama Shore cast excluding him from their MTV Video Music Awards plans in 2019 came to a head in the first half of season 4. 

Gus and Jeremiah got into another physical altercation. Later, Gus ran away from the Montana house for a brief period. But by the end of the season, Gus and Jeremiah seemed to put their differences aside. Does this mean their feud is over? Or will they need another hot tub heart-to-heart in the Athens, Georgia house? 

Does Nilsa Prowant have to leave Athens because of her pregnancy? 

Nilsa welcomed her son Gray Allen Gazda with her fiancé, Gus Gazda, on May 20, 2021. “[Thirty-one] hours of labor to get you here and I would do it all over again just for you,” she wrote on Instagram.

Nilsa is still very pregnant in the second half of season 4. In the season 4B premiere, she mentions how close her doctors are to their new filming location in Athens. Will Nilsa be able to stay the entire length of their vacation, or will her trip be cut short by going into labor? 

Will Aimee Hall open up about her breakup in ‘Floribama Shore’ Season 4B? 

Floribama Shore star Aimee Hall was in a relationship with Dillon Johnson until recently. In the season 4 premiere, Aimee shares she and Dillon broke up. 

As Aimee explains in the episode, Dillon was lying to her about his drug use. Will she open up more to her roommates about her breakup with Dillon in this season of Floribama Shore?

Does the ‘Floribama Shore’ cast mention Kortni Gilson in season 4B?

Kortni Gilson hasn’t been part of the Floribama Shore cast since season 3. She left the series to address her mental health. 

Later, Kortni revealed to Dr. Drew Pinsky she was raped as a teen. Today, Kortni is sharing details of her mental health journey with her Instagram followers. She’s still not back to filming for Floribama Shore, but some fans hope she’ll turn up at some point this season. 

Will Kortni appear in season 4B, or will the Floribama Shore cast mention her at some point? Tune in to new episodes every Thursday on MTV to find out the answers to these questions and more.
