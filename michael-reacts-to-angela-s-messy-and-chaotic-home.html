Michael Ilesanmi is getting a first look at his new home, and it’s not quite what he expected. 

In the next episode of 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After? Season 8, Michael’s wife Angela Deem gives him a tour of her house after he finally arrives in America from Nigeria. But the state of the accommodations leaves Michael in shock. 

Michael moves in with Angela in the next episode of ‘90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After?’

Michael and Angela met online and married in 2020. But due to his visa struggles, they’ve spent all their years together in a long-distance relationship. Until now. Michael has finally received his spousal visa, which allows him to move to the United States to be with Angela. It’s the end of a long journey for the couple, and the start of a new one as husband and wife living in the same country. 

In a clip from the July 7 episode of 90 Day Fiancé (via X), Angela shows Michael his new home. 

“It’s getting late,” she says. “I’m just giving you a quick tour because I’m exhausted.” 

She then opens the door to her bedroom, which is cluttered with piles of clothes, stuffed animals, and other items. Michael is stunned. 

“Angie. Oh my goodness,” he says. “Do you sell clothes here or what?”

Angela starts to apologize for the mess and pull items off the bed. 

“I need your help,” she snaps at her husband. 

“I got you baby,” he replies. “With time, we’ll arrange it all. Don’t worry.” 

Michael wonders if there’s room for him in Angela’s home

In an interview with producers, Michael opens up about how he felt seeing Angela’s home for the first time. 

“Oh my God. Why everywhere is clothes, clothes. And where am I gonna put my things?” he says as Angela laughs beside him. 

“I’ve been in a hotel many times with my wife,” he adds. “But I never realized it was this messy. But I’m so excited to spend my first night with my wife, Angie.”

Angela is also looking forward to her next chapter with Michael. She’s hoping that being in the same country will improve their sex life. But there won’t be any romance happening right away. 

“Not tonight,” she says. “I am exhausted. There’s a do not enter sign on my vagina.”
