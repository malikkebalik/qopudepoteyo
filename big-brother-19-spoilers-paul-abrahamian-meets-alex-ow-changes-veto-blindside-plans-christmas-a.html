Big Brother 19 Spoilers: Paul Abrahamian Meets Alex Ow, Changes Veto Blindside Plans Christmas A

 Posted on September 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  413 words  |  Martina Birk

Big Brother 19 spoilers reveal another blindside could rock Josh Martinez’s game on Wednesday’s special eviction. Paul Abrahamian has all the power with the Veto around his neck. Alex Ow has a chat with the BB18 vet, and she tried to convince him to save her and send Christmas Abbott to the Jury house.

To recap Week 11 so far, Josh won the HOH competition and put Alex and Kevin Schlehuber on the block. Paul won the Veto and planned to save Alex from the chopping block. However, after thinking about it, he wonders if he should send Alex home next and evict Christmas next. If Paul decides to use the POV and save Alex, the only person that can be nominated is Christmas. That is what makes using the Veto tricky for Paul.

Around 3 a.m. on Sunday morning, Paul told Christmas that he would have a chat with Alex about the POV. He feared how Alex would react and asked her to help him with the backlash. Christmas agreed and seemed happy that he would be moving forward with the plan to stick with her and Kevin to the end.

Big Brother 19 spoilers state that Paul’s plan started to change after he had a chat with Alex. Apparently, she pitched a fit and cried at the thought of going to Jury before Kevin, Christmas and “cry baby” Josh. She reminded Paul they had a deal and demanded he honors it.

Paul seemed upset about her harsh words and told her that he is playing to win and needs to do what is best for his game. She understood that and said even if he doesn’t use the Veto on her and sends her to Jury, he has her vote. She added that she would talk to Jason Dent and try to get him to vote for Paul, too.

After Paul and Alex ended their conversation, they hugged, and she told him to do whatever is best for his game. Paul seemed conflicted and wasn’t sure what he should do. BB19 spoilers state that Paul could flip the game and send Christmas to the Jury house if he wants.

So, what does Paul want to do? No one truly knows so it will be a surprise to everyone in the house. Do you think Paul Abrahamian will use the Veto during Monday, September 11 POV ceremony? Stick with CDL for more Big Brother 19 spoilers, news, and live feed updates!
